# VPN Complexity

No documentation can cover every VPN setup. The combinations are endless:

- Which VPN client (OpenVPN, WireGuard, etc.)
- Which torrent client (qBittorrent, Transmission, etc.)
- Which VPN provider (PIA, Mullvad, etc.)
- Whether you use a combined VPN/torrent container or separate containers

VPN configuration is outside the scope of Mousehole. Mousehole is one piece of a
larger puzzle — if the other pieces aren't working, Mousehole can't help. The
examples here are starting points, not exhaustive guides.

Understanding [sidecar containers](./sidecars.md) will take you a long way. When
you get stuck, the
[forum post](https://www.myanonamouse.net/f/t/84712/p/p1013257) and LLMs are
both good resources.
